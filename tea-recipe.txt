Ingredients:
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tea bag of your choice

Directions:
1. Pour the milk into a vessel and place it on a burner and heat until it boils.

2. Now switch of the burner and pour the milk from the vessel to a cup.

3. Add sugar to the cup and stir it.

4. Now dip the tea bag into the cup and enjoy your tea.
